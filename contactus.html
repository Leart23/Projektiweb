About

At Alpine Ski & Snowboard, the online ski and snowboard store, of the Alpine group, we offer ski clothing, ski pants, snow boots for all types of skiing such as ski mountaineering, and cross-country skiing, ski alpine, snowboard, Nordic skiing, Telemark. We stock the best brands: Salomon, The North Face, Atomic, Dakine, Dynafit, Head, Columbia, Bergans, Eider, Roxy, K2, Salewa, and more. We have at your disposal a wide range of ski products that adapt to the whole family and to all levels, from Freestyle skiers, cross-country skiers or beginners.

WHERE IS Alpine Ski & Snowboard LOCATED?

Alpine Ski & Snowboardis part of the Alpine group, located in Prishtine (Prishtine, Kosovo). Alpine Ski & Snowboard is an online store specialized in the sale of sports equipment. From the Alpine Ski & Snowboard facilities located in Kosovo, all kinds of sports products are shipped all over the world

FROM WHICH COUNTRIES CAN YOU BUY AT Alpine Ski & Snowboard ?

From all over the world! Through Alpine Ski & Snowboard you can buy all kinds of sports equipment and we send it directly to your home or to a collection point, wherever you are in the country.
Some of the countries we ship to are Germany, United States, Albania, United Kingdom, France, Italy, Switzerland, Denmark, Sweden, Norway and Russia.
